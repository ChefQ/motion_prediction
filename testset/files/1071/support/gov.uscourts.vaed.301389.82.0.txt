DEFENDANTS’ MOTION TO DISMISS PLAINTIFFS’
CONSOLIDATED AMENDED COMPLAINT

Defendants Lumber Liquidators Holdings, Inc., Robert M. Lynch, Daniel E. Terrell,
Thomas D. Sullivan, and William K. Schlegel hereby move under Federal Rule of Civil
Procedure 12(b)(6) to dismiss all claims brought against them by Plaintiffs. For the reasons
more fully set forth in the Memorandum of Law in Support of this Motion, the Plaintiffs’
Consolidated Amended Complaint for Violations of Federal Securities Laws, ECF No. 80, fails
to state claims upon which relief can be granted, as it does not satisfy the applicable pleading
standards. In support of this Motion, Defendants submit a Memorandum of Law, a Declaration
of Lyle Roberts, and supporting exhibits that may be considered in ruling on this Motion to
Dismiss.
