PLAINTIFF GREAT LAKES EXPLORATION’S APPLICATION FOR
TEMPORARY RESTRAINING ORDER AND PRELIMINARY INJUNCTION
Pursuant to Fed. R. Civ. P. 65, Plaintiff Great Lakes Exploration, LLC, by
counsel, respectfully moves the Court to grant a temporary restraining order and
preliminary injunction restraining the Intervenors from utilizing information provided by
Plaintiff regarding the precise location of the artifacts believed to be part of the
Defendant shipwreck for (a) the purpose of taking, or attempting to take, actual
possession or custody of any such artifacts, and (2) for any purpose other than asserting
and protecting their interests in this proceeding. In addition, Plaintiff respectfully renews
its request that the Court forthwith issue a warrant for arrest of the Defendant and/or such
portions of the Defendant as may be placed in the custody of the U.S. Marshal pursuant
to Supplemental Rule C., Fed. R. Civ. Proc.
In support of this Application, Great Lakes Exploration adopts the analysis and
authorities in the attached Memorandum in Support.
