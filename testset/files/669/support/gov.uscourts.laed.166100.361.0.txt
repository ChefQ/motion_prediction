MOTION FOR PRELIMINARY ORDER OF FORFEITURE
NOW INTO COURT comes the United States of America, by and through the
undersigned Assistant United States Attorney, who respectfully submits this motion for a
preliminary order of forfeiture against the defendants, Walter Reed and Steven Reed, jointly and
severally in the amount of $78,539.11, and against the defendant, Walter Reed, in the amount of
$609,489.57, pursuant to the Notice of Fraud Forfeiture and Notice of Money Laundering
Forfeiture contained in the Superseding Indictment (Rec. Doc. 64, pp.28-31) for the reasons set
forth in the attached supporting memorandum. Pursuant to Rule 32.2(a) of the Federal Rules of
Criminal Procedure, “the court must determine the amount of money that the defendant will be
ordered to pay” and, at sentencing, enter either a specific order of forfeiture fixing the amount of
the judgment or a general order of forfeiture that “will be amended under Rule 32.2(e)(1) when
. . . the amount of the money judgment has been calculated.”
