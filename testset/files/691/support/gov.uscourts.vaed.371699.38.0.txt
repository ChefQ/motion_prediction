DEFENDANTS’ MOTION TO DISMISS THE
FIRST AMENDED COMPLAINT
Under Federal Rules of Civil Procedure 12(b)(1) and 12(b)(6), Defendants B. Perrotte, T.
Osborne, D. Hun Yim, P. Manneh, and A. Nichols move this Court to dismiss Plaintiffs Mynor
Abdiel Tun-Cos, Jose Pajarito Saput, Luis Velasquez Perdomo, Pedro Velasquez Perdomo,
Germán Velasquez Perdomo, Eder Aguilar Aritas, Eduardo Montano Fernández, Nelson Callejas
Peña, and José Cárcamo’s claims against them because this Court lacks subject matter
jurisdiction over those claims, and because Plaintiffs have failed to state a claim upon which
relief may be granted. This motion is based on the accompanying memorandum. A proposed
order is attached.
